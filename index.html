How This Works:

This is a website that gathers posts from different users, shows their reactions and the comments to the posts.
Clicking on a user profile will display their information (restricted to name, username, email and phone).

These are the following tabs that are currently available:

  • "Home": the current window where you can navigate to different pages.
  • "Posts" where you can see an archive of posts and their respective comments, reactions and author.
  • "Contact us": A form you can fill out that requires you to enter a username, an email (@ and . required) and a tickbox that is required to be checked before submitting.
